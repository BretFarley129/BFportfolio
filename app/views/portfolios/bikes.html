Bicycle Marketplace

GitHub

A "Craigslist-lite" application I made. Titled "Bicycle Marketplace", users can register, list bikes that they are willing to sell, edit said bikes and view bikes that other users have posted. In addition to having fully verified registration, it also features acount lockout to deter fradulent login. Every 24 hours, a new "Bike of the day" is selected automatically and featured on the login page. When browsing for bikes, one can filter by the name of the bike to quickly find the exact bike they are looking for. If you are interested in potentially purchasing a bike you can click the contact button to display the sellers contact information.
